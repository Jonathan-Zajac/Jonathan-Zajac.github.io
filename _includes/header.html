Computational Biophysics & Molecular Design

Hi there! I'm Jonathan. I am a computational molecular scientist interested in the structure, function, and dynamics of biomolecular systems. I develop and leverage methods combining statistical mechanics, thermodynamics, machine learning, and enhanced sampling to investigate complex systems.

Currently, I am a 5th year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Chemistry at the University of Minnesota, advised by Professor Sapna Sarupria. My research focuses on studying protein-solvent interactions to design stable biological formulations.
